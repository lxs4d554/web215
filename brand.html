Where it's okay to show off!

What is this all about?

This website is a fun and friendly place where kids can show off their creative talents. These days kids are constantly plugged into some type of device (or want to be if they're not). This site is focused on celebrating what happens when you turn off the devices.

How is it done?

When you kids put down the devices, you really soar. I've seen some amazing talent out there, and I want to show it off for you. Do you play a sport? Write poetry? Play an instrument? Dance? Whatever you do when you're not plugged in, I want you to tell me about it. Write it out, send me a picture, and maybe you'll see it featured on Tweens Uplugged!
